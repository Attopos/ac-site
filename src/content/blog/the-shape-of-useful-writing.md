Useful writing does not merely display ideas. It compresses them. The reader should leave with stronger distinctions, not just stronger impressions.

That usually means less ornament and more structure: a clean claim, a tension worth resolving, and a sequence that earns its conclusion.

For a personal blog, this matters even more. The site is not just a container. It is evidence of how the author thinks.
